About Me

From Pain to Purpose: My Healing Trajectory

For me, I was abruptly shaken by a double blow of autoimmune diseases: Ankylosing spondylitis (a form of arthritis affecting joints and the spine) and Type 1 diabetes. The physical pain from arthritis was overwhelming, and I started noticing blood sugar issues and the need for insulin. My health and doctors were starting to dictate my quality of life. I knew I needed to take control before being riddled with ailments became my norm. Determined to heal myself, I embarked on a path of self-discovery and empowerment.

By connecting with a naturopathic specialist, I delved into regenerative detoxification, iridology, herbalism, natural hygiene, and fasting protocols, gaining a deep understanding of the body's functioning and the role of elimination organs and glands. Through implementing this knowledge, I was able to achieve my goal and stay insulin-free for 101 days with the support of daily herbal treatments. Within six months, pancreatic regeneration was initiated and I achieved optimal blood sugar levels.

Through years of intensive training and self-healing, I not only restored my health but also found my calling to assist others on their healing journeys.

My desire to help people led me to become a certified health coach, specializing in regenerative detoxification, iridology, herbalism, and fasting protocols.

The Deeper Healing: Integrating Hypnotherapy and Spiritual Exploration

Life's trajectory is never linear. While I successfully worked on healing my physical body, I faced a traumatic incident, triggering a toxic family dynamic that affected my emotional well-being. Consequently, I needed insulin again.

This episode highlighted the complexity of emotional health and its impact in the overall well-being of any individual. I realized that emotional and mental healing was equally crucial and often more complex to holistic healing. This realization led me to explore the fascinating realms of hypnosis and hypnotherapy, where I deep dived into emotional healing, trauma processing, and subconscious reprogramming.

Leaning and Enlightenment

My personal experiences ignited a deep desire to discover the secrets of trauma healing, reprogramming the subconscious mind, and understanding the complex layers of emotions.

In my pursuit, I've immersed myself in numerous hypnotherapy courses, delving into age regression techniques and trauma work. I studied one of the most renowned hypnotherapists in the world, Cal Banyan, who equipped me with profound insights and skills I've used to help complex clients with significant breakthroughs.

But my quest didn't stop there; it took a metaphysical turn that opened the door to spirituality and energetics. Embracing metaphysical hypnosis, I discovered the captivating realm of energy clearings and past life healing. The concept of a life between lives fascinated me, whereas souls, we consciously choose our appearances, settings, and environments on Earth to fulfill our unique purpose and soul growth. From this foundation, I delved further into the depths of metaphysics, quantum physics, and all things spiritual. I welcomed energy healing and psychic practices into my repertoire, recognizing their powerful potential to facilitate thorough transformations and spiritual awakenings.

Even as I pursued my studies in hypnosis and hypnotherapy, my inquisitive nature led me to understand the subtle language of dreams, the voice of the subconscious mind. For four years, I dedicated myself to the study of dream interpretation, enabling me to grasp the intense messages embedded within our dreams.
